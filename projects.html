COMAP 2025 - Wear Simulation

Feb 2025
This was my submission for COMAP 2025, where my team and I earned our second honorable mention. Given a staircase with unique dimension and material, we constructed an acceptance-rejection simulation from a n-modal mixture model combined with Monte-Carlo techniques to predict wear over time. We then created a reverse technique by comparing the Kernel Densnity Element to the mean to find and invert the 1σ distance.
